Supporting you with the right care

Auckland Surgical Associates and Surgical Weight Solutions are led by High Volume and Experienced surgical partners Dr Suheelan Kulasegaran and Dr Michael Booth, both widely regarded as leaders in their field. Together, they bring more than 30 years of combined experience across General, Upper Gastrointestinal, and Bariatric surgery, supported by an extensive record of academic publication and active involvement in research and teaching.

Their expertise spans laparoscopic, robotic, and open surgical techniques. Dr Suheelan Kulasegaran and Dr Michael Booth have significant experience in both primary and complex revisional laparoscopic surgery. Both surgeons are tertiary referral surgeons for Complex Bariatric and Upper Gastrointestinal cases.

Dr Suheelan Kulasegaran also performs advanced and complex Upper GI and Bariatric revisional procedures using laparoscopic and robotic techniques. He performed New Zealand's first total robotic oesophagectomy and gastrectomy.

We prioritise offering a world class and premium service for our patients.

Please be aware that some procedures require a referral from your GP. If you're unsure whether a referral is needed, you're welcome to contact the clinic for guidance.
